Retail & Warehouse Operations — Conversational Inventory

AI Agent for Inventory & Order Management

Live scenario canvas — Make.com

The Problem

Rins Retail Co. managed inventory in a central Google Sheet, but warehouse staff were rarely at a desk — checking or updating stock from a phone browser was slow and error-prone. Any fix also had to reliably tell a simple lookup apart from a request to change inventory, since a mistake in either direction could mean an oversold item or an inaccurate count.

What I Built

StockFlow AI, an AI Agent that sits inside Slack, interprets plain-language requests, and decides whether to search, update, or add a new item in the inventory sheet. A stock check ("do we have any blue widgets left?") gets an instant lookup and reply; a stock update ("just received 50 red widgets") gets located, adjusted, and confirmed back in the same thread — all without opening a spreadsheet.
