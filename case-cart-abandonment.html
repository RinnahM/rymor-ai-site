High-Ticket Funnels — Cart Abandonment Recovery

High-Ticket Cart Abandonment Savior

System flow — trigger to recovery, fully automated in GoHighLevel

The Problem

Up to 70% of high-intent prospects drop off during checkout on high-ticket funnels. Standard checkout tools don't preserve a visitor's contact details once they leave mid-session, so every one of those near-buyers vanished with no way to follow up — wasted ad spend, inflated customer acquisition costs, and a sales team with zero visibility into who almost bought.

What I Built

A behavioral interception system built entirely inside GoHighLevel. The moment someone submits the order form and doesn't complete checkout, a 20-second delay gives them a real chance to finish on their own — the "behavioral golden window." If they don't, a personalized SMS goes out automatically, linking them straight back to their live checkout session. At the same time, the contact gets tagged in the pipeline and an internal alert fires to the sales team, so a real person can follow up in real time instead of finding out days later that a high-ticket lead slipped away.
