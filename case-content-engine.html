System flow — scheduled, unattended, cross-platform

The Problem

Content creators and businesses making short-form video content were stuck editing and publishing everything by hand — writing prompts, generating clips, checking renders, and posting one at a time across platforms. A week's worth of content took a week to make.

What I Built

A scheduled pipeline where Gemini generates a structured video prompt on its own, authenticates via OAuth2 and calls a video generation API, then polls until each render is ready, filters out failed generations, and converts the finished clips for publishing — landing automatically on both Facebook and YouTube through their respective APIs, with zero manual steps in between.
