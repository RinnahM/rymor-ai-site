About

I'm a B.S. Computer Science graduate who made a deliberate pivot into AI automation — building systems for service businesses that answer the phone at 2am, remember every past job, and publish content without anyone touching a keyboard.

What sets my work apart is that I've sat on the other side of the desk. Before automation, I worked as a social media manager and virtual assistant, doing the repetitive manual work these systems now replace — so I know firsthand exactly where a business bleeds time. I write a clear SOP for every system I build, so you're never left dependent on me to understand your own operations. My toolkit sits at the intersection of workflow orchestration and applied AI: n8n, GoHighLevel, Zapier, and Make for the plumbing, Claude and Gemini for the reasoning. The goal is never "add more AI" — it's fewer missed leads, faster response, and hours back on the calendar every week.

I work with busy business owners who are ready to stop running on manual processes — the kind who want to streamline operations, buy back their time, and save real money by letting systems handle what used to eat their week.
