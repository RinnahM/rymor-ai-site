B2B SaaS — Inbound Lead Qualification

AI Agent: Sales Outreach & Meeting Booking

Live scenario canvas — Make.com

The Problem

FlowState CRM's founder was spending more than three hours a day manually working every inbound inquiry — researching the prospect's company, checking calendar availability, drafting a personalized reply, and coordinating with a part-time rep over Slack. That overhead capped how many leads the small team could realistically follow up with, and slowed down the leads that mattered most.

What I Built

An AI Agent scenario that acts as an autonomous first-line sales development rep. The moment a new inquiry lands in Gmail, the agent reads it, researches the prospect's company via web search using their email domain, checks the founder's Google Calendar for open slots over the next three days, and drafts a personalized reply in Gmail that addresses the prospect's likely needs with proposed meeting times. A Slack notification lets the team know a lead is qualified and a draft is ready to review and send.

The Results (First 30 Days)

12 minLead response time, down from ~4.5 hours
95%Faster response to inbound leads
1 hr/wkManual admin time, down from 15 hrs/week
+77%Increase in meeting booking rate (22% → 39%)
